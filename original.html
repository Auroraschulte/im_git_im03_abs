How is the data generated?

These are the average values for each weekday over the last 30 days. These are based on real-time pedestrian counts taken hourly in the city of Lucerne.

'Calm' indicates fewer than 30 people per time unit, 'moderate' indicates 30–80 people, and 'busy' indicates 80 or more people. These thresholds are intended as a guide only and do not replace live forecasts.
